What is Animals

Forms of Existence

Animals are not characters.

They are forms of existence — like creatures, like objects, somewhere in between.

Survival Strategies

Each form reflects a way of surviving.

Spreading, floating, branching — inspired by real biological principles, abstracted into form.

"There are many ways to exist,
and every form has its own reason to live."
