What is Animals

Designed for this experience

animals are artworks built around Phantom Graph.

They are not fully explained. Not always moving. Instead, they are slightly incomplete, slightly strange, and open to interpretation.

“Somewhere between what is shown
and what is imagined,
something feels alive.”

Not characters. Forms.

animals are forms of existence — like creatures, like objects, somewhere in between.

  • float
  • connect
  • detach
  • pulse
  • change

Completed by the viewer

Because they are slightly incomplete, your mind begins to fill the gaps.

animals are completed inside the viewer’s mind.

How to begin

Look at multiple images.
Notice the space between them.
Stay there for a moment.

If something begins to move in your mind,
you are already experiencing Animals.
